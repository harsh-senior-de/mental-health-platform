## CONSTRAINT-016 — All v1 Sessions Are Zoom Video Only; Initial Assessment Is Video-Mandatory

## CONSTRAINT-017 — List C Drugs Prohibited from Telemedicine Prescribing

source: specs/001-patient-psychiatrist-match/spec.md (FR-044)
type: nfr
List C drugs (including but not limited to alprazolam, diazepam, lorazepam, nitrazepam,
chlordiazepoxide, zolpidem, methylphenidate, modafinil, phenobarbitone, and depot
antipsychotics) are legally prohibited from telemedicine prescriptions under the
Telepsychiatry Operational Guidelines 2020 and the Drugs and Cosmetics Act 1940.

bypass mechanism exists. The List C list is maintained in PlatformConfiguration.

automated enforcement in v1. List B reference list maintained in PlatformConfiguration.

## CONSTRAINT-019 — Patient Data Portability and Deletion Within Defined SLAs

source: .specify/memory/constitution.md + specs/001-patient-psychiatrist-match/spec.md
type: nfr
Users must be able to export and delete their data at any time. On-demand deletion must be
processed within 72 hours. Data export must be delivered within 72 hours via WhatsApp (if
enabled) and SMS. Download links expire after 48 hours. The export package includes: intake
responses, all approved session notes, all issued prescription PDFs, appointment history,
notification preferences, and the patient's own SessionFeedback records. Raw session
transcripts are excluded from the export (intermediate artifact, not formal clinical record).
Clinical records are anonymised (not deleted) and retained for 7 years from the session date.
The 72-hour delivery window satisfies both DPDPA 2023 and MHCA 2017 Form A (15-day) timelines.

## CONSTRAINT-029 — Form B-1 Compliance; CareRecommendation Extended

source: specs/001-patient-psychiatrist-match/spec.md (FR-015b, GAP-034 resolution)
type: schema
The CareRecommendation entity (session record) serves as the platform's MHCA 2017 Form B-1
equivalent. It must capture all Form B-1 mandatory fields: presenting complaints summary,

nominated rep), GAP-044 (MSE free-text), GAP-046 (Subjective SOAP), GAP-047 (identity

## CONSTRAINT-030 — Crisis Helplines Always Visible; Platform Is Not a Crisis Service

source: specs/001-patient-psychiatrist-match/spec.md (FR-042)
type: nfr
The platform is not a crisis intervention service and does not provide emergency psychiatric
care to first-time users, consistent with Telemedicine Practice Guidelines 2020. National
crisis helpline numbers must be displayed permanently on the login/registration page, the
booking page, and the patient dashboard — visible to all users at all times, whether logged
in or not. Helpline numbers and the iCall WhatsApp number are stored in PlatformConfiguration.
The Urgent Review session type is available only to returning patients (at least one prior
completed Initial Assessment) and is not a general-purpose emergency pathway.
gaps resolved: GAP-037 (crisis pathway is helplines, not platform session type)
